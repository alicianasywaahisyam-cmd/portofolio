PawSure: Animal Healthcare Service Application

An integrated animal healthcare service application with comprehensive features including consultations, appointment booking, home visit scheduling, and online medicine purchases.

About the Project

PawSure was my 4th-semester project for the Information System Analysis and Design course. This application is designed as an animal healthcare system, focusing on system design through UML (use case diagrams, activity diagrams, sequence diagrams, etc.) and a UI/UX prototype. PawSure supports various features such as user registration & login, consultation scheduling between pet owners and veterinarians, and management of pet medical records.
